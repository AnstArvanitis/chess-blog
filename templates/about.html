About Me

This is what I do.

Welcome to The Grandmaster's Log. They say that chess is the gymnasium of the mind, but I believe coding comes pretty close. Both require patience, strategy, and the ability to foresee the consequences of every move.

This project is powered by Python and Flask. While utilizing a Bootstrap theme for the layout, I focused on building a robust Backend: implementing secure User Authentication, Relational Databases (SQLAlchemy), and dynamic content management.

It was developed as part of my 100 Days of Code journey, demonstrating my ability to integrate complex technologies into a fully functional application.
